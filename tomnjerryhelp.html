HOW TO PLAY ?

This game is played using mouse cursor.
One tile is moved using drag and drop process.
Tiles are switched with a blank tile adjecent to the picture tile.
Tiles can be moved in any direction like up,down,left,right. Tiles cannot be moved diagonally or selecting any other tile from the board.
